STEP LMS Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how STEP (ST Claret College's Learning Management System) collects, uses, and protects your personal information. We are committed to ensuring the privacy and security of our users' data.

Information We Collect: We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Identification Information: This includes your name, student ID, email address, and other contact details provided during registration.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with STEP, such as courses you enroll in, your progress, assignments submitted, and communication within the platform.
  • Technical Information: This may include your IP address, browser type, device information, and operating system to help us optimize the platform and ensure security.

How We Use Your Information: We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the STEP LMS platform and its features.
  • To manage your account and learning progress.
  • To communicate with you regarding course updates, announcements, and support.
  • To personalize your learning experience and recommend relevant content.
  • To analyze platform usage and improve our services.
  • To ensure the security and integrity of the platform.

Data Security: We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Data Retention: We will retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide you with our services and as required by applicable laws and regulations.

Your Rights: You may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including the right to access, correct, or delete your data. Please contact us if you have any such requests.

Changes to this Privacy Policy: We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review it periodically.
